Our Training Curriculum

Comprehensive learning methodology covering all aspects of safe driving

Module 1: Fundamentals

Vehicle controls, dashboard functions, mirrors adjustment, seating position, and pre-driving checks.

Module 2: Basic Skills

Starting engine, accelerating, braking, steering, turning, parking, and basic maneuvers.

Module 3: Road Rules

Traffic signs, traffic rules, right of way, lane discipline, and regulatory compliance.

Module 4: Safety Skills

Defensive driving, hazard perception, emergency handling, and accident prevention techniques.

Module 5: Advanced Techniques

Highway driving, night driving, different weather conditions, and complex traffic situations.

Module 6: Test Preparation

Mock tests, examiner interaction, test route familiarization, and confidence building sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many classes do I need?

It depends on your skill level. Beginners typically need 8-10 classes, while refresher courses require 4-5 classes. We customize based on your needs.

Can I choose my own instructor?

Yes! You can request your preferred instructor based on their expertise and availability. We'll match you accordingly.

What documents do I need?

You need a learner's license, valid ID, and proof of address. Our team will guide you through the documentation process.

Do you cover the test route?

Absolutely! We familiarize you with the RTO test route and conduct mock tests on those exact routes.

What if I fail the test?

We offer free refresher classes to identify weak areas and help you prepare for the next attempt. Our 95% success rate speaks for itself!
